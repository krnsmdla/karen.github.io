LEATHER BANDS

There's nothing quite like the feeling of putting on a leather watch band. From the initial break-in period to feeling the material wear and form to your wrist, your leather band chronicles your watch's life story. Which leather band will you choose to tell yours? Picking a color for your watch strap is a good place to start. Traditionalists might go for one of the classics: black, brown, or tan. While we don't find anything wrong with a timeless leather watch band, we also like to keep things fresh. Nixon leather also comes in a variety of vegetable-tanned color options. This process allows us to bring vibrancy and style to the leather watch band category.

RUBBER BANDS

$40

If working up a sweat is a regular occurrence for you, then a rubber watch band is the perfect accessory. Our rubber watch bands come in a few different materials, but all of them are weather-resistant, durable, and crazy comfortable. The first option is our FKM synthetic rubber. This watch band material has a heavier feel but has excellent temperature resistance. The second option is the traditional silicone watch bands. Silicone watch bands are lighter and more flexible than rubber watch bands, but what you choose comes down to personal preference. Sport bands like these work well on both digital and analog watches. Swapping out a stainless steel band for a silicone or rubber replacement band can not only give you a performance benefit but can change up the look of your watch, too. We offer rubber replacement bands for both men's and women's watches, giving everyone on the move access to this sporty upgrade.

SUSTAINABLE BANDS

$30

Being environmentally conscious in material choices is a high priority for Nixon. We've done extensive research to find the right partners to help us make quality watch bands out of alternative materials. Our sustainable watch band offering comprises three different materials: pineapple leather, #TIDE recycled plastics, and standard recycled plastics. Pineapple leather is a relatively new material made from cellulose fibers extracted from pineapple leaves, PLA, and resin. This PETA-approved, vegan material can do anything natural leather can do, including making beautiful eco-friendly watch straps. The second is our #TIDE recycled plastic nylon. For this watch band material, we exclusively use plastic waste destined to wind up in the ocean. By partnering with local seaside recycling programs, we can get the material needed to make stylish and durable recycled watch bands while keeping our ocean playground cleaner. Finally, we use standard RPET (recycled plastic) for other nylon watch straps. While the raw, recycled material comes from a broader range of sources, it still reduces our watch bands' environmental impact. Our sustainability efforts don't stop here, and with your support, we can continue to look for innovative ways to take care of our planet while still making great products.
